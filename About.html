Mission

To deliver high-quality education within the T and T community and beyond, ensuring that every learner has the opportunity to achieve their full potential.

Our Objectives

At T and T Junior School, we are dedicated to:

  • Developing all-round competent children who will become future leaders in Uganda.
  • Providing a wide range of mentoring and skilling services to enhance the personal growth of our learners.
  • Partnering with parents to ensure sustainability at every stage of a child’s development.
  • Preparing students for success in both academics and life beyond the classroom.

Our Staff and Community

Our team comprises over 50 qualified staff members, representing a wide range of professional backgrounds. With their dedication and the support of the wider community, we strive to offer a safe and nurturing environment for all learners, regardless of their gender, age, or background.
